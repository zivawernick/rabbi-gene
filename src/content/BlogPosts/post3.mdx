It has been several years since Reverend Dr. Martin Luther King Jr's " I have a Dream" speech.

Unfortunately it seems his dream is farther from reality than it was when first spoken.
Nevertheless if you don't dream it can never happen.

So I decided to dream.

I dream of a time when all the inhabitants of Israel will live in peace and equality, no matter their origin, nationality or religious beliefs. I have a dream that a sovereign, independent Palestinian state will exist in peace and cooperation with Israel.
I have a dream that Christians, Moslems and Jews will celebrate the common origins of their faiths and will work together to establish peace and goodwill throughout the world.
I have a dream that every child no matter to whom they are born will receive the medical care, early childhood development and an education that will give them pride in themselves and be worthwhile contributors to advancing civilization.
Dreams can come true, all it takes is the will to make it so!
