## Preface


It has been some decades past the Holocaust and the establishment of the Jewish
State. And once again antisemitism has raised its head abetted from political
forces on the left as well as the right.

The Jewish people briefly united in most part after the Holocaust have fallen back
into the divisiveness that has been one of its traits from time past.

It is my objective in this survey of the history of this people to attempt to identify the
ideas and mode of being that have kept this people separate from others eliciting
both pride in their being and hatred from enemies who from ancient times
resented a “people that will dwell alone.”
